ABOUT ME

I am an indoor person who likes to spend his time reading. I like reading about geopolitical issues, news and analysis. I also enjoy reading about new technologies and domestic politics. I am particularly fond of science fiction. I can read for hours on end if a book catches my fancy.
Another hobby of mine is music. I like listening to music and playing my guitars. Currently I have 2 acoustic guitars and an electric guitar too. I enjoy spending time plucking the strings on idyllic afternoons. Nowadays, I am more into fingerstyle. Currently, I am learning the song Let Her Go by Passenger.
Another hobby of mine is Japanese anime. I am, by all means, an otaku. I have more or less watched all of the essentials and a whole lot more. Even in anime, I prefer science-fiction followed closely by shonen. As an otaku, I can also claim to be an avid gamer. I currently boast of having multiple PlayStation consoles. I like single player story-driven games in contrast to the popular trend of playing massive multiplayer games.

Despite being an indoor person, I dream of traveling the world. The only reason I want to be a developer is so that I can earn more and get a work from home job so that I can travel to my heart's content. I want to see this world before I can't. I, currently, live with my two cats, Honey and Caramel.

My life had been pretty dark before they came along. Now I am happy to have them with me on this joruney, shedding light(literally!) through this cold, dark, wasteland of eternity.
